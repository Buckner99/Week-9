My Compound Bow

I first purchased my Bow in February of 2023. I have practiced a ton, and have been going up in strength each week. Right now I can pull back 80lbs.

Competition

Every week on Friday we have a competition shoot at a local bow shop. We shoot 30 arrows in 10 rounds. There are 3 targets on one sheet of paper shooting one arrow at each target, and that counts for a round.

For Fun

I like going out in the woods on the weekends and finding a random stump to shoot. I have to guess how far away it is and adjust my site on my bow to that range. It is a challenging experience, and familiarizes yourself with depth perception.
